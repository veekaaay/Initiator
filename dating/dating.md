❤️ Dating One-Liners
Light-hearted and charming — great for first dates or new connections.


"So what's your controversial food opinion? Mine is that cereal is a soup."
"If you could live in any city for a year, no strings attached — where are you going?"
"What's something you're weirdly proud of that won't show up on a resume?"
"Tell me something about you that I'd never guess just by looking."
"What's your ideal lazy Sunday? I need to know what I'm working with here."
"Are you a 'plan everything' person or a 'figure it out when we get there' person?"
"What's the last thing that genuinely made you laugh out loud?"
"If we had to do something fun right now that isn't dinner, what are we doing?"
