💼 Networking One-Liners
Professional but friendly — perfect for conferences, meetups, and work events.


"What's the most interesting thing you've worked on lately?"
"I've already had three cups of coffee. What's keeping you going today?"
"What brought you to this event — curiosity or obligation?"
"If you could explain what you do to a five-year-old, how would that sound?"
"I'm trying to meet one genuinely interesting person tonight. Are you my answer?"
"What's one thing about your job that would surprise most people?"
"I promise I won't pitch you anything. Unless you want to hear about my cat."
"Best career advice you ever ignored?"
