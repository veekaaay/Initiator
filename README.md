🗣️ Initiator
One-liners to break the ice and keep the conversation going.
Ever been stuck in an awkward silence? Initiator gives you fun, clever, and easy one-liners for small talk and ice breaking — whether you're at a party, networking event, first date, or just meeting someone new.

💡 What is this?
Initiator is a collection of conversation starters organized by situation. No more awkward pauses — just pick a category and go.
